Abstract

We outline four live scenarios for cosmic origins relative to "seeding": (i) natural self-generation, (ii) civilizational genesis by earlier minds, (iii) simulation, and (iv) we are early/first. Each admits observable footprints or decision-relevant bounds. We restore a detailed catalog of mathematical encoding options (carriers, codes, statistics, capacity/robustness, and selection formalism), propose concrete, falsifiable programs, and argue for œÑ-robust messages if "first" remains plausible.
